A Chrome extension that lets you export your complete Swedish medical journal with one click. Your data stays on your device.

What It Does

📋

One-Click Download

A floating button appears on 1177.se. Click it to automatically expand and download all your journal entries.

📄

Structured EIR Format

Exports your records in the open EIR format (YAML) - machine-readable, human-readable, and easy to import into health apps.

🔒

100% Private

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to any server. Zero tracking, zero analytics.

🌐

Transfer to Eir.Space

Optionally view your records in the Eir.Space viewer for AI-powered insights and better visualization of your health data.

📊

Complete History

Automatically clicks "Load More" to fetch your entire medical history - vaccinations, diagnoses, visits, prescriptions, and more.

🇬🇧

Swedish Healthcare

Built specifically for the Swedish 1177.se patient portal (Journalen). Understands Swedish medical record structures.

How It Works

1

Install the Extension

Download and load the extension in Chrome via Developer Mode.

2

Log in to 1177.se

Navigate to journalen.1177.se and authenticate with BankID as usual.

3

Click Download

Click the floating "Download Journals" button that appears on the page.

4

Get Your Files

Receive a .txt and .eir file with your complete structured medical records.

Installation

Since this extension isn't on the Chrome Web Store, install it manually:

  1. Download the latest release from GitHub Releases, or clone the repo.
  2. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/
  3. Enable Developer mode (toggle in the top right).
  4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder.
  5. Navigate to journalen.1177.se, log in, and use the floating button.

Privacy First

We take the privacy of your medical records extremely seriously. This extension is designed so that your health data never leaves your device.

The EIR Format

Your records are exported in a clean, structured YAML-based format:

# EIR File Format v1.0 metadata: format_version: "1.0" source: "1177.se Journal" patient: name: "Your Name" entries: - date: "2025-03-17" category: "Vaccinationer" provider: name: "Your Healthcare Provider" content: summary: "TBE-vaccination" notes: - "Dos: 0.5 ml"
